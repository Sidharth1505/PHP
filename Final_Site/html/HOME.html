About Us

We strive to provide the best experience to our guests. We opened our first cafe in 2015 at sector-17 in Chandigarh. Coffee Hilltop to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee .It's been an exciting journey since then to becoming the largest organised retail cafe chain in the country. We’re also present in Austria, Czech Republic and Malaysia, so if your travel takes you there, do stop by our outlets to get a taste from back home!
